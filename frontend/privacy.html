Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Last updated: June 13, 2026

1. Introduction

DevOps Academy Egypt ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our students and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at devopsacademy.cloud-stacks.com or enroll in our courses.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you:

  • Enroll in a course: Full name, email address, phone number, selected course, and experience level.
  • Request a service: Company name, contact details, and project description.
  • Contact us: Any information you provide via WhatsApp, email, or contact forms.

We do not collect payment card details directly — all payments are processed through secure third-party services (bank transfers, Vodafone Cash, InstaPay).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process your enrollment and manage your course registration
  • Communicate with you about schedules, updates, and course materials
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send you relevant course announcements and promotional offers (you can opt out)
  • Improve our website, courses, and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Instructors: Your name and email may be shared with course instructors for classroom management.
  • Service providers: We use third-party services for hosting (Azure), email delivery, and website analytics.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted to our website
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security updates and monitoring of our infrastructure
  • Data stored in encrypted databases with access controls

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Specifically:

  • Enrollment data: Retained for 3 years after course completion for certification verification.
  • Contact inquiries: Retained for 1 year.
  • Account data: Retained until you request deletion.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Delete your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the details below.

8. Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for functionality. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising networks. We may use basic analytics to understand website traffic and improve user experience.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (GitHub, LinkedIn, WhatsApp). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify enrolled students of significant changes via email. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised.
