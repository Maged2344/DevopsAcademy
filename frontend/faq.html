Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DevOps Academy Egypt

General Questions

What is DevOps Academy Egypt?
DevOps Academy Egypt is the leading DevOps training provider in Egypt. We offer comprehensive, hands-on courses taught by industry experts (including Microsoft Certified Trainers) to help professionals transition into DevOps engineering roles or advance their careers.
Who are your instructors?
Our instructors are industry professionals with real-world experience. Our lead instructor is a Microsoft Certified Trainer (MCT) with years of experience in DevOps, cloud computing, and infrastructure automation. All instructors actively work in the field and bring real project experience to the classroom.
Do I need prior experience to enroll?
It depends on the course. Our Linux Fundamentals and Git courses are designed for complete beginners. The full DevOps Engineering program starts from zero and builds up systematically. However, intermediate courses like Kubernetes and Terraform assume basic Linux and Docker knowledge.
What makes you different from other training providers?
We focus on hands-on, project-based learning — not just theory. Every student builds real infrastructure, writes real pipelines, and deploys real applications. Our curriculum is aligned with industry certifications (RHCSA, AWS CCP, CKA) and updated regularly to match market demands.

Courses & Curriculum

What courses do you offer?
We offer individual courses in Linux, Docker, Kubernetes, CI/CD (Jenkins, GitHub Actions), AWS & Multi-Cloud, Terraform & Ansible, Git, DevSecOps, and Monitoring. We also offer a comprehensive 26-week DevOps Engineering Program that covers all topics end-to-end with a capstone project.
How long is the full DevOps program?
The full DevOps Engineering Program is 26 weeks (approximately 6 months), with 4-hour sessions. It covers Linux, programming, Docker, Kubernetes, CI/CD, cloud computing (AWS, Azure, GCP, Alibaba, Oracle), Terraform, Ansible, monitoring, DevSecOps, and a capstone project.
Are courses online or in-person?
We currently offer both online (live instructor-led via Zoom) and in-person sessions at our training center in Egypt. All sessions are recorded and shared with students for review.
Do I get a certificate after completing a course?
Yes! Upon completing any course or program and passing the final assessment, you receive a DevOps Academy Egypt certificate. For the full program, you also get career support including portfolio review, resume building, and interview preparation.
What cloud providers do you cover?
We provide deep-dive AWS training (aligned with Cloud Practitioner certification) plus fundamentals of Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud Platform (GCP), Alibaba Cloud, and Oracle Cloud. Our approach is cloud-agnostic — you'll learn to deploy on any provider.

Enrollment & Payment

How do I enroll?
You can enroll directly from our Courses page. Fill out the enrollment form with your details and preferred course. Our team will contact you within 24 hours to confirm your spot and provide payment details.
What are the payment options?
We accept bank transfers, Vodafone Cash, InstaPay, and cash payments. We also offer installment plans for the full DevOps program — pay in 2-4 installments with no interest. Contact us via WhatsApp for payment arrangement details.
Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?
We offer a full refund if you withdraw within the first week (first 2 sessions) of any course. After that, we can transfer your enrollment to a future batch or apply the remaining balance to another course.
Are there any discounts available?
Yes! We offer early-bird discounts (10% off if enrolled 2 weeks before the batch starts), group discounts (3+ people), and student discounts with a valid university ID. Follow our social media for seasonal promotions.

Technical & Career

What tools will I learn?
You'll gain hands-on experience with: Linux (RHEL/CentOS), Git/GitHub, Docker, Kubernetes, Helm, Jenkins, GitLab CI, GitHub Actions, ArgoCD, Terraform, Ansible, AWS, Azure, GCP, Prometheus, Grafana, ELK Stack, SonarQube, Trivy, and more.
Do you help with job placement?
Yes! Our full DevOps program includes career support: resume/CV optimization, LinkedIn profile review, GitHub portfolio building, mock interviews, and referrals to our hiring partner network. We have a 95% job placement rate within 3 months of graduation.
What certifications should I pursue after the program?
We recommend: AWS Cloud Practitioner (CLF-C02), Red Hat Certified System Administrator (RHCSA), Certified Kubernetes Administrator (CKA), and HashiCorp Terraform Associate. Our curriculum aligns with these certification objectives.
What's the expected salary for a DevOps engineer in Egypt?
Junior DevOps engineers in Egypt typically earn EGP 15,000–25,000/month. Mid-level engineers earn EGP 30,000–50,000/month. Senior/remote positions can reach EGP 80,000+ or $2,000–5,000/month for international remote roles.
